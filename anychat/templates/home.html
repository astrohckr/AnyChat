Local People

When you use Anychat, you will have access to group chats made by people around you. Joining a chat may mean seeing someone you know, maybe an old friend, possibly an acquaintance, who knows. Or, maybe not. You may meet some really cool people while you are here.

settings

Private and Secure

If anyone wants our data, we have nothing to give out. User accounts are just an id and coordinate pair tied to a session. In other words, when you clear your session and localStorage data, your account is deleted. In the case that you do send us something private, communication is encrypted with modern TLS (HTTPS).
